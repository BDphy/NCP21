Bangladesh Physical Society

National Conference on Physics – 2021

6 – 7 August 2021

This site contains the list of poster presentations with videos and posters presented at the National Conference on Physics – 2021 organized by Bangladesh Physical Society. The two days long conference was held online from 6 August to 7 August in the year of 2021. The videos can also be watched from the YouTube Playlist. The program details of the conference and the published abstract book on occasion of the conference can also be downloaded from here. For the conference purpose the site was created by Dr. Mohammad Abdur Rashid, a member of the organizing committee.
